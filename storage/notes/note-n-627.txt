<div style=""><h2><span>Components Introduction</span><br></h2></div><div>Component based development (CBD)&nbsp;</div><div>Design and create larger units in a software units (Components)</div><div><ul><li>Components are specialised in different parts of the domain.</li><li>Components provide services to each other via an interface.</li><li>Components hide their internals.&nbsp;</li></ul><div><div><span style="font-weight: bold;">Characteristics of a component</span></div><div><ul><li>Is an autonomous unit within a system.&nbsp;</li><li>Hides it's internals.</li><li>Can be reused.</li><li>Can be replaced.</li></ul><div><span style="font-weight: 700;">Characteristics of a logical component</span><br></div><div><ul><li>Cluster of classes that cover an area of competence inside the domain.&nbsp;</li><li>UML term subsystem</li></ul></div><div><span style="font-weight: bold;">Characteristics of a physical component</span></div></div><div><ul><li>Executable unit of program code.</li><li>Contains a coherent set of services.</li><li>Accessible only via a consistent facade/interface.</li><li>UML term Component</li></ul></div></div></div><div><br></div><h2>Determine the services of a logical component</h2><div>There are two service types:</div><div><span style="font-weight: bold;">Frontend services:</span></div><div><ul><li>Provides functionality to the end users</li><li>Contains the GUI and are task specific</li><li>Not reusable</li></ul><div>Examples:&nbsp;</div><div><ol><li>Register course type</li><li>Plan course&nbsp;</li><li>Process course booking</li></ol></div><span style="font-weight: bold;">Backend services:</span></div><div><ul><li>Provides business wide generic functionality: retrieve and stored data or check business rules.&nbsp;</li><li>Supports the user services</li><li>Reusable&nbsp;</li></ul><div>Examples:</div></div><div><ol><li>Get all courseTypes</li><li>Get all courses of type X</li><li>Create new course</li></ol></div><h2>Recognise Services</h2><div>Frontend&nbsp;</div><div><ul><li>Each usecase results in a frontend service</li><li>The frontend service belongs to the logical component whose entities are updated/created/deleted.</li></ul><div>Backend</div></div><div><ul><li>All services called from a fronend service to a business domain (domain model)</li><li>All services called from another logical component to this business domain.</li></ul><div><br></div></div><h2>Een volledig componenten
